# 08 Final Ranking

Theme: AI data center power infrastructure

Status: completed

No High Priority Research candidates. MYRG is the recommended first deep-dive target because it combines electrical/interconnection relevance, positive FCF, and less extreme valuation than more crowded equipment names.

## Rankings
1. MYRG - Watchlist - MYRG is a focused electrical contractor with utility T&D and C&I/data-center adjacency, positive FCF, and less extreme sales multiples than the most crowded equipment names.
2. POWL - Wait for Pullback - POWL has the strongest filing-level evidence for backlog, switchgear/power systems, and data-center diversification, but valuation and momentum show substantial repricing.
3. MOD - Wait for Pullback - MOD has hard filing evidence that Data Centers are 35% of fiscal 2026 net sales and offers CDUs/rear-door/immersion-adjacent solutions, but FCF is negative and valuation/momentum are stretched.
4. ATKR - Watchlist - ATKR is cheaper and cash-generative with electrical infrastructure exposure, but data-center-specific validation is weaker and the products may be more commoditized.
5. BDC - Watchlist - BDC has direct data center and Smart Infrastructure evidence with reasonable valuation versus crowded power names, but it is more network/connectivity than core power infrastructure.
6. IESC - Watchlist - IESC has data-center and electrical services exposure with positive FCF, but valuation and price momentum are already high and exact data-center backlog is missing.
7. AEIS - Wait for Pullback - AEIS has power-conversion relevance and AI/energy-efficiency exposure, but market data shows high valuation and direct data-center commercial validation is incomplete.
8. VICR - Wait for Pullback - VICR has technically relevant vertical power delivery for AI accelerators, but valuation is extremely stretched and commercial validation is not yet enough for a core thesis.
9. GNRC - Observe - GNRC could benefit if data centers adopt more backup/on-site power, but current evidence is more scenario optionality than proven AI data-center revenue.
10. FLNC - Observe - FLNC has grid-storage relevance to power-constrained loads, but direct AI data-center validation is weak and FCF remains negative.
